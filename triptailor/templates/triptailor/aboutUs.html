About Us!

Trip Tailor is a marketplace to aid prospective travelers in finding local guides in a dream destination city! Sign up as a user to pick and choose which tour is perfect for you or a group of friends. If you are interested in giving tours of your very own home town, sign up as a guide! Guides create tours according to what restaurants, landmarks, and entertainment venues they are familiar with. As a guide, you may set your own date, time, price, and group size. After you are finished taking a tour, feel free to leave a review for your guide!
